ABOUT ENYO TRADE

Trading built on a system, not chance.

Enyo Trade prioritizes sustainable execution quality over short-term excitement. Every decision is evaluated through setup quality, market structure, risk-to-reward, prop firm rules and the account's current drawdown position.

Our objective is not simply to pass a challenge. It is to develop a repeatable operating model capable of protecting funded accounts and pursuing payout and scaling opportunities with discipline.

01

Who are we?

An independent trading brand operating evaluation and funded accounts obtained through third-party prop firm programs.

02

What do we do?

We combine prop firm selection, challenge planning, risk control, journaling and performance review within one disciplined process.

03

What do we not do?

We do not sell funded accounts, provide investment advice or manage third-party funds, brokerage accounts or trading accounts.

RISK MANAGEMENT

Define the downside first.
Then evaluate the trade.

Success in prop trading is not only about choosing the right direction. It means keeping the account in the game while managing daily loss limits, maximum drawdown and open-position exposure at the same time.

01

Pre-defined riskThe maximum acceptable loss is established before entering the trade.

02

Controlled position sizingSize is adjusted to volatility, stop distance and account limits.

03

Drawdown protectionRisk is reduced during losing periods to prevent revenge trading and overtrading.

04

Daily stop ruleTrading stops when the daily threshold is reached or decision quality deteriorates.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Clear questions.
Clear answers.

We describe Enyo Trade's operating model and its boundaries openly.

Is Enyo Trade a prop firm?+

No. Enyo Trade does not sell challenges or funded accounts. It carries out independent trading activity on evaluations and funded accounts acquired through third-party prop firm programs.

Do you manage client funds or accounts?+

No. Enyo Trade does not manage third-party money, investment accounts, brokerage accounts or trading accounts.

Do you provide investment advice or signals?+

No. The content on this website is intended to present the Enyo Trade brand and explain its independent trading approach. It is not investment advice or a trading signal.

How will performance be presented?+

Performance, challenge and payout information will only be shared when it can be verified, together with the relevant period and risk context. Past results do not guarantee future performance.
