ABOUT ENYO TRADE

Built for longevity, not noise.

Enyo Trade prioritizes calm, repeatable execution over short bursts of excitement. Every decision is filtered through setup quality, market structure, risk-to-reward, prop firm rules and the account's current drawdown position.

The objective is not merely to pass a challenge. It is to build an operating standard that protects funded accounts, produces credible performance and earns the right to scale.

01

Who are we?

A selective trading desk operating third-party evaluations and funded accounts under a clearly defined execution model.

02

What do we do?

We combine prop firm selection, evaluation planning, risk control, journaling and post-trade review into one disciplined workflow.

03

What do we avoid?

No fund management, no third-party account handling and no performance theatre dressed up as credibility.

RISK MANAGEMENT

Protect the account first.
Then earn the right to scale.

Prop trading is not just directional accuracy. It is the ability to stay inside daily limits, defend drawdown and keep exposure under control while waiting for quality setups.

01

Pre-defined riskThe maximum acceptable loss is established before entering the trade.

02

Controlled position sizingSize is adjusted to volatility, stop distance and account limits.

03

Drawdown protectionRisk is reduced during losing periods to prevent revenge trading and overtrading.

04

Daily stop ruleTrading stops when the daily threshold is reached or decision quality deteriorates.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Direct questions.
Direct answers.

We explain the Enyo Trade model clearly, including what we do and what we deliberately do not do.

Is Enyo Trade a prop firm?+

No. Enyo Trade does not sell challenges or funded accounts. It conducts independent trading activity on evaluations and funded accounts acquired through third-party prop firm programs.

Do you manage client funds or accounts?+

No. Enyo Trade does not manage third-party money, investment accounts, brokerage accounts or trading accounts.

Do you provide investment advice or signals?+

No. The content on this website is intended to present the Enyo Trade brand and explain its independent trading approach. It is not investment advice or a trading signal.

How will performance be presented?+

Performance, challenge and payout information will only be shared when it can be verified alongside the relevant period and risk context. Past results do not guarantee future performance.
